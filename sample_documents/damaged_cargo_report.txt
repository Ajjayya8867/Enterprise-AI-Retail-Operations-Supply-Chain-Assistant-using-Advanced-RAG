INCIDENT REPORT: DAMAGED CARGO AT RECEIVING DOCK
Report Reference: IR-2026-089
Date of Incident: June 26, 2026
Location: Warehouse-B (Receiving Dock 4)
Inspector: Marcus Vance, Shift Lead

1. SHIPMENT DETAILS
- Purchase Order: PO-2026-044
- Supplier: ScanTech Solutions
- Carrier: SwiftCargo
- Date of Arrival: June 26, 2026 (08:45 AM)
- Total Pallets: 6 Pallets

2. DESCRIPTION OF DAMAGE
During offloading of the cargo, Pallet #3 (containing ScanTech Standard Handheld Label Printers, SKU-671-9P) was observed to be heavily tilted. Upon closer inspection:
- The outer shrink-wrap was torn, and the structural integrity of the outer cardboard box was compromised.
- Water damage was visible on the bottom layer of boxes, indicating exposure during transit.
- 12 units of SKU-671-9P had crushed chassis, and 8 units failed to power on due to internal moisture damage.
- Total damaged units: 20 units.
- Total estimated value of damages: $2,400.00.

3. CAUSE ANALYSIS
The carrier, SwiftCargo, utilized a trailer with a leaking roof panel. Heavy rain during transit on Interstate 90 resulted in water ingress. Furthermore, the pallet was improperly secured with insufficient strapping, leading to shifts and crushing during braking.

4. ACTIONS TAKEN & SLA REMEDIES
- Receiving lead rejected the 20 damaged units.
- The remaining 5 pallets (comprising 180 units) were successfully scanned into inventory at Warehouse-B.
- Form 802-Damages was signed by the SwiftCargo driver, acknowledging the transit moisture.
- A damage return claim has been filed with ScanTech Solutions. Under section 3.3 of the SLA, ScanTech must dispatch replacement units within 5 business days.
- A SwiftCargo handling penalty fee of $150.00 has been logged.
