NEXUS RETAIL OPERATIONS POLICY MANUAL
Document Code: OP-POLICY-Q4-2026
Topic: Q4 Peak Season Inventory & Audit Protocols

1. HOLIDAY PEAK SEASON TIMELINES
The Q4 holiday peak season begins on November 1, 2026, and extends through January 5, 2027. 
- During this window, standard receiving hours are extended to 24/7 operations at Warehouse-A and Warehouse-B.
- Warehouse-C will maintain standard receiving (06:00 AM - 10:00 PM) but will run triple-shifts for order fulfillment.

2. INVENTORY COUNT & PHYSICAL AUDIT SCHEDULE
To maintain data accuracy, physical counting schedules are modified as follows during Q4:
- Bi-Weekly Cycle Counts: Mandatory cycle counts must be performed every second Tuesday of the month for all "Category A" (High-Value / Tech) inventory items.
- Next Full Physical Audit: The comprehensive end-of-year physical inventory audit is scheduled for December 28, 2026. All inbound receiving will be paused from 08:00 AM to 06:00 PM on that day.
- Audit Coordinators: 
  * Warehouse-A: Chief Auditor Elena Rostova
  * Warehouse-B: Operations Manager Robert Chen
  * Warehouse-C: Lead Coordinator Marcus Vance

3. RETAIL HOLIDAY RETURN PROTOCOLS
All customer returns received at physical retail stores during the holiday period must be consolidated and shipped back to Warehouse-B.
- Return Inspection: Returns must be inspected within 48 hours of arrival at Warehouse-B.
- Restocking Rules: Items in unopened packaging are to be restocked immediately with status "In Stock". Damaged or opened returns must be written off or sent to the liquidations bay.

4. SAFETY & COMPLIANCE
- Anti-Fatigue Aisle Mats (SKU-554-3M) must be placed in all packing stations at Warehouse-A and Warehouse-B.
- Reflective Safety Vests (SKU-772-2Q) are mandatory for all employees, visitors, and truck drivers on receiving dock floors.
